Our values

At Terry Johnson Trucking, Inc. we maintain core values of integrity, reliability, wisdom, quality, sustainability, and safety.

Serving the Central Coast since 1978

Terry Johnson Trucking, Inc. is family owned business that has been serving the central valley of California since 1978. Our primary business is hauling rock, sand, gravel, asphalt and extends to other services such as vacuum trucks, water trucks, off road equipment, roll off bin rentals and lowbed services. Our newest business venture is Triple J Ready Mix, a 109 state certified concrete batch plant located in Coalinga.

Our trucking company has four locations to conveniently and efficiently serve our patrons. Our main office, the hub of Terry Johnson Trucking, Inc. is located in Coalinga. Our other locations include Hanford, Porterville, & Santa Maria. All of our locations are strategically located near aggregate & asphalt facilities and clean water sources.

Our goal is to provide quality transportation of materials and superior customer service. Give us a call for any of your trucking needs.
